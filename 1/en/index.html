イントロダクション

After rescuing the Cherubim, Mint,
who you find passed out by the side of the road
following her exile from Heaven,
she moves in with you.

Your close friendship naturally blossoms
into something more,
and she becomes your girlfriend.

There's never a dull moment with the cheerful,
naively honest, and rambunctious Mint by your side,
which is only amplified when Lily,
a half-demon and close friend of Mint's,
suddenly shows up on your doorstep
in all her over-the-top flirtatious glory

NAME

Mint

CAST
飴川紫乃

She is a Cherubim:
A half-animal, half-angel girl.
She has cat-like ears and tail, as well as angelic wings but does not have halo. She has a complex about her tiny wings and usually wears toy wings to make up for it. She isn't good at using her angelic powers and is still an angel-in-training.

She came down from Heaven to find a certain something but is unsuccessful and ends up collapsing on the side of the road. That’s when you find and save her. Soon after, she begins to fondly call you her master and affectionately waits at home for your return each day.

She's kind of a brat who loves to play tricks. She wants to repay the favor of you saving her but it ends up being her that is taken care of instead.

NAME

Lily

CAST
立花沙羅

A half-animal, half-demon girl.
She has animal-like ears and a tail, as well as black wings. She has the demonic power to make people unaware of her presence (she very easily blends in with her surroundings). Possessing a great body, she uses that to tease Mint.

Wanting to help pay her way while living with you and Mint, she hides her true idenity in order to take on a part-time job. She likes to have fun and joke around, but deep down is a serious girl who is always willing to give her honest opinion and offer advice on anything.

NAME

Cacao

CAST
水野七海

She is a Cherubim: a half-animal, half-angel girl.
She has cat-like ears and tail, as well as angelic wings but unlike Mint, she does have a halo. She excels as an angel and is proficient in magic. However, just like Mint she isn’t a full-fledged angel yet.

After her big sister, Mint, never returned after saying she was going down to Earth, Cacao grew worried and went to look for her. Upon learning why Mint is on Earth, she struggles desperately to change her sister’s mind.
